# MyCloset
App for organizing and keeping track of your items with creating outfits based on your existing items, sharing them with friends and getting outfits seuggestions from friends.  

 The app let you organize you items by closets adding any info with a picture. Search and filter with own created tags. Build your outfits for every event using your own existing items. Share your saved outfit and allow access to your friends so they can suggest any outfits or changes to one of your shared ones.
 You can request suggestion for outfit with any requirements from Stylists, accept or decline and also leave rating for it.
